Web Development

It is a group competition.The group should contain not less than 5 members.

This competition involves three rounds.

1.Idea Submission(Online)

2.Powerpoint Presentation(Online)

3.Project Submission(Offline)

Problem statement:You are supposed to identify problems in your society and develop solution to the problem identified.You can use any type of framework and web technologies.The project has to be done in the given time.

Top three winners will be awarded with cash price.(detailed information will be updated soon).All participants will get participation e-certificates.



App Development

It is a group competition.The group should contain not less than 5 members.

This competition involves three rounds.

1.Idea Submission(Online)

2.Powerpoint Presentation(Online)

3.Project Submission(Offline)

Problem statement:You are supposed to identify problems in your society and develop solution to the problem identified.Then develop an app.The project has to be done in the given time.

Top three winners will be awarded with cash price.(detailed information will be updated soon).All participants will get participation e-certificates.



Technical Quiz
